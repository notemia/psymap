Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PsyMap ("the App," "we") handles information.

1. Data Collection

PsyMap does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on servers. The app works fully offline. There are no accounts, no registration, and no backend.

2. On-Device Data

The app stores on your device only: your list of favorite books and language settings. This data never leaves the device and is not synced.

4. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs. We do not track your activity.

5. Age and Children

This app is rated 16+. It is not directed at children and does not collect data from children.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. The current version is always available on this page.

7. Contact

For privacy questions: support@psymap.app
