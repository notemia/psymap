FAQ

Does the app work offline?
Yes. All content is bundled. No internet required.
Do I need an account?
No. Data (favorites) is stored only on your device.
Can I use it for diagnosis?
No. The app is a study reference. It is not intended for diagnosis, treatment, or patient care.
How do I restore my subscription?
Open Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. Use the same Apple ID.
What devices are supported?
iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.
